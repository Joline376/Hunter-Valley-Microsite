About Us

Welcome to Hunter Valley! Situated in New South Wales, just north of Sydney and extending inland from the coastal town of Newcastle, the Hunter Valley is one of Australia’s most iconic wine regions, renowned for its rich wine-making heritage and beautiful landscapes. Our Goal is to enhance the visitor experience while highlighting the region’s rich heritage, world class wines and unique cultural attractions.

We are dedicated to showcase all that Hunter Valley has to offer from exquisite wine tours and gourmet food to beautiful walks and local artwork. Whether you are a wine lover or seeking a peaceful getaway, Hunter Valley has something for everyone. Join us in exploring this breathtaking region of Australia, and allow us to assist you in making lifelong memories.
